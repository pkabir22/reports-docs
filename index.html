This is ABL's most critical and complex daily report — a real-time command center for the Treasury and Capital Markets teams' liquidity management, covenant compliance, and capital markets decision-making.
Purpose & Overview
A comprehensive daily snapshot of ABL's capital structure and liquidity position. Covers real-time warehouse utilization, liquidity runway, funding mix, and covenant compliance across all warehouses and securitization shelves.
Report Sections
Section 1 — Capital Structure & Liquidity Snapshot
Daily overview of total indebtedness, available capacity, leverage ratios, blended cost of capital, and ending cash position.
Section 2 — Securitizations (2 RTL Shelves)
Balances, cash held in payment/accumulation accounts, UPB surplus/shortfall, and associated funding costs per securitization shelf.
Section 3 — Warehouse Financing (Multi-Bank)
Facility limits, collateral pledged, outstanding balances, PVE, funding costs, and covenant status per lender/warehouse.
Section 4 — Daily Cash Flow Tracker & 29-Day Projection
Ties operating cash & PVE to funding activity, capital markets actions, payoffs, payroll, ending liquidity, and covenant coverage for the next 29 work days.

let
    source = CommonDataService.Database("abl1.crm.dynamics.com"),
    query = "
    select distinct
        o.name as 'Property Address',
        cast(pr.new_processing_projected_close_date as date) as 'TOE Closing Date',
        o.new_dealstagename as 'Deal Stage',
        o.new_visableupb as 'UPB',
        case
            when o.new_loanpurposename like '%New Construction%'
                then (-0.5 * o.new_visableupb)
            else (-0.15 * o.new_visableupb)
        end as 'Haircut'
    from opportunity as o
    left join new_processing as pr on o.opportunityid = pr.new_opportunity
    left join new_postclose as p on o.new_servicingfile = p.new_postcloseid
    where o.statecodename = 'Open'
        and pr.new_processing_projected_close_date >= cast(getdate() as date)
        and o.new_dealstagename in ('Clear To Close', 'Final Underwriting', 'Processing')
        and LOWER(o.name) NOT LIKE '%test%'
",
    response = Value.NativeQuery(source, query),
    #"Sorted Rows" = Table.Sort(response,{{"TOE Closing Date", Order.Ascending}})

let
    source = CommonDataService.Database("abl1.crm.dynamics.com"),
    query = "
    SELECT
        s.new_loannumber1 as 'ln_id',
        o.new_los_property_address1 as 'address',
        s.new_spdate as 'ln_sold_date',
        o.new_loanamount as 'ln_amount',
        CASE
            WHEN o.new_escrowagentname = 'ABL RPC Dutch'
                THEN o.new_loanamount
                ELSE (
                    o.new_cashavailableforhousepurchaseatclose
                    + s.new_drawfundsdisbursed
                    + o.new_interestescrow
                    + o.new_escrowother
                    - s.new_blanketpropertypaydown
                )
        END AS 'upb',
        s.new_upb as 'crm_upb',
        o.new_cashavailableforhousepurchaseatclose as 'day1_upb',
        s.new_loanownername as 'owner',
        CASE
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Construction%' THEN 'Ground Up'
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Rehabilitation%' THEN 'Fix & Flip'
            ELSE 'Bridge'
        END AS 'ln_type',
        CASE
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Stabilized%' THEN 'ARM'
            ELSE 'Fixed'
        END AS 'ARM Flag',
        CASE
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Purchase%' THEN 'Purchase'
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Cash Out%' THEN 'CO Refi'
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename LIKE '%Loan Refinance%' THEN 'RT Refi'
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename = 'Refinance & New Construction' THEN 'RT Refi'
            WHEN o.new_loanpurposename IN ('Property New Construction Only',
                 'Property Rehabilitation Only') THEN 'RT Refi'
            ELSE ''
        END AS 'ln_purpose',
        REPLACE(o.new_escrowagentname,'ABL RPC','') as 'escrow_type'
    FROM opportunity as o
    LEFT JOIN new_postclose as s
        ON o.new_servicingfile = s.new_postcloseid
    WHERE s.new_loanownername = 'ABL2024RTL1'
        AND o.new_dealstagename <> 'Paid Off'
",
    myQuery = Value.NativeQuery(source, query)
in
    myQuery

let
    source = CommonDataService.Database("abl1.crm.dynamics.com"),
    query = "
    select
        'ABL RPC Residential Credit Acquisition LLC' as 'Lender',
        cast(s.new_loannumber1 as int) as 'Loan Number',
        case
            when o.new_loanpurposename like '%Construction%' then 'Ground Up'
            when o.new_loanpurposename like '%Rehabilitation%' then 'Lender Funded Rehab'
            else 'Bridge'
        end as 'Loan Program',
        o.new_borrowingentity as 'Business Entity Borrower Name',
        o.new_guarantor1lastname as 'Guarantor Last Name',
        o.new_guarantor1firstname as 'Guarantor First Name',
        case
            when o.new_loanpurposename like '%Cash Out%' then 'Cash Out'
            when o.new_loanpurposename like '%Purchase%' then 'Purchase'
            else 'Refinance'
        end as 'Loan Purpose',
        o.new_trueclosingdate as 'Note Date',
        o.new_loanamount as 'Original Loan Amount',
        o.new_ltsinterestrate / 100 as 'Original Interest Rate',
        (o.new_cashavailableforhousepurchaseatclose
         + s.new_drawfundsdisbursed
         + o.new_interestescrow
         + o.new_escrowother
         - s.new_blanketpropertypaydown) as 'Current Loan Amount',
        o.new_los_property_address1 as 'Property Address',
        o.new_los_property_city as 'Property City',
        o.new_ltspropertystatenname as 'Property State',
        o.new_propertyzipcode as 'Property Zip',
        o.new_ficotruemid as 'Orig Borrower FICO',
        CASE
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated >= 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%SINGLE%FAMILY%'
                AND COALESCE(o.new_rehabamount,0) >= 0.5 * COALESCE(o.new_ltsasis,0)
            THEN 'DQ/ SFR/ Heavy Rehab'
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated >= 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%SINGLE%FAMILY%'
                AND COALESCE(o.new_rehabamount,0) < 0.5 * COALESCE(o.new_ltsasis,0)
            THEN 'DQ/ SFR/ Rehab'
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated >= 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%SINGLE%FAMILY%'
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_loanpurposename,'')) NOT LIKE '%REHAB%'
            THEN 'DQ/ SFR/ Bridge'
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated < 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%SINGLE%FAMILY%'
                AND COALESCE(o.new_rehabamount,0) >= 0.5 * COALESCE(o.new_ltsasis,0)
            THEN 'SFR/ Heavy Rehab'
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated < 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%SINGLE%FAMILY%'
                AND COALESCE(o.new_rehabamount,0) < 0.5 * COALESCE(o.new_ltsasis,0)
            THEN 'SFR/ Rehab'
            WHEN s.new_daysdelinquentcalculated < 30
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_ltspropertytypename,'')) LIKE '%MULTI%FAMILY%'
                AND UPPER(COALESCE(o.new_loanpurposename,'')) NOT LIKE '%REHAB%'
            THEN 'MFR/ Bridge'
            ELSE NULL
        END AS '1Sharpe_Classification'
    from opportunity as o
    left join new_postclose as s on o.new_servicingfile = s.new_postcloseid
    left join contact as c on o.new_guarantor1lu = c.contactid
    where s.new_loanownername in ('1Sharpe (Warehouse)', '1Sharpe (Whole Loan)')
        and o.new_dealstagename <> 'Paid Off'
",
    myQuery = Value.NativeQuery(source, query)

    myQuery
Step-by-Step Production Process
  1. Refresh Dynamics CRM Power Queries — open the master workbook and run "Refresh All" to pull the latest data into all CRM-connected tabs.
  2. Load CFSB Daily Summary — confirm Treasury has uploaded the latest Daily RPC Sheet to Dropbox. Load via Get Data › From Excel Workbook.
  3. Load Nomura EOD Report — confirm upload to Dropbox, load the Results tab.
  4. Enter Mizuho EOD figures — open the Mizuho end-of-day email and enter figures manually into the 1_Pager.
  5. Calculate and enter Payoffs — open each Investor Notification Deposit PDF. Principal Payment col (6th from right) × 22.5%, sum all → cell L23.
  6. Enter Teams channel data — check Teams for DSCR Sale/Repo, RTL/Trust activity, and other capital/funding activity.
  7. Enter Operating Cash — CFSB portal: SUM(Acct 5247 + Acct 5239 + Mor Dutch Esc + Acct 5352).
  8. Enter RTL1 PA & AA figures — USB Pivot portal: enter Payment Account and Accumulation Account balances for 2024-RTL1 and 2025-RTL1.
  9. Verify formula propagation — confirm all data has flowed correctly into the 1_Pager.
  10. Validate covenant & liquidity figures — review covenant cells, cross-check ending cash, WH utilization, and PVE.

ABL 2025-RTL1 Weekly Report Weekly
Capital Markets & Securitization Team · ABL2025RTL1 Email Distro · Produced Tuesday for prior week
▼
Weekly surveillance report for the ABL 2025-RTL1 securitization trust — covering new loan activity, active pool health, sub-limit compliance, delinquency, and payoff/repurchase pipeline. Published as a PDF each Tuesday and circulated to the ABL2025RTL1 email distro.
Purpose & Overview
Tracks the weekly activity and ongoing performance of the ABL 2025-RTL1 RTL securitization trust. Sent each Tuesday covering the prior Monday–Thursday. On a monthly basis, the report is supplemented with time-series trend charts showing pool balance vs. theoretical, DQ/default rate trends, and cumulative turnover since trust inception.
Report Sections
Section 1 — Weekly Activity
Three side-by-side panels: Gross Activity (new adds), Payoff Activity, and Employee/Repurchase Activity — each showing unit count and loan amount for the week.
Section 2 — Active Pool & Sub-Limit Breakdown
Active 2025-RTL1 population: total units, aggregate loan amount, UPB. Full sub-limit table covering WAC, WALTV, WALP, loan type, LTV bands, occupancy, state/borrower concentration, and trust eligibility caps.
Section 3 — Sold Not Disposed & Payoff Requested
Loans sold but not yet disposed in the trust system, and loans with an active payoff request — with pool-level stats and loan-level detail tables.
Section 4 — Paid Off This Week
Loan-level detail of all payoffs processed during the report week: address, city, state, UPB, WAC, LTC, LTV, experience tier, Non-SLC flag.
Section 5 — Delinquency (DQ 30+ / 60+ / 90–120+)
Three DQ buckets each with pool stats and a full loan-level table — days DQ, % redeemed to date, maturity date, loan purpose, deal stage, and servicer notes.
Section 6 — Repurchased
Loans repurchased from the trust — unit count, aggregate UPB, and loan-level detail consistent with the DQ sections.
Monthly Add-On — Time-Series Trend Charts
Sent monthly alongside the weekly report: (1) Actual vs. Theoretical Trust Balance, (2) DQ 60+ Rate & Default Rate over time, (3) Cumulative Turnover since trust inception (7/2025).

Covers new loan activity, active pool health, sub-limit compliance, delinquency, and payoff/repurchase pipeline. Published as a PDF each Tuesday and circulated to the ABL2024RTL1 email distro.
Purpose & Overview
Tracks the weekly activity and ongoing performance of the ABL 2024-RTL1 RTL securitization trust. Follows the same layout and report structure as the 2025-RTL1 Weekly Report — sent each Tuesday covering the prior Monday–Thursday. On a monthly basis, the report is supplemented with the same time-series trend charts (Actual vs. Theoretical Balance, DQ/Default Rate, Cumulative Turnover).
Report Sections
Section 1 — Weekly Activity
Three side-by-side panels: Gross Activity (new adds), Payoff Activity, and Employee/Repurchase Activity — each showing unit count and loan amount for the week.
Section 2 — Active Pool & Sub-Limit Breakdown
Active 2024-RTL1 population: total units, aggregate loan amount, UPB. Full sub-limit table covering WAC, WALTV, WALP, loan type, LTV bands, occupancy, state/borrower concentration, and trust eligibility caps.
Section 3 — Sold Not Disposed & Payoff Requested
Loans sold but not yet disposed in the trust system, and loans with an active payoff request — with pool-level stats and loan-level detail tables.
Section 4 — Paid Off This Week
Loan-level detail of all payoffs processed during the report week: address, city, state, UPB, WAC, LTC, LTV, experience tier, Non-SLC flag.
Section 5 — Delinquency (DQ 30+ / 60+ / 90–120+)
Three DQ buckets each with pool stats and a full loan-level table — days DQ, % redeemed to date, maturity date, loan purpose, deal stage, and servicer notes.
Section 6 — Repurchased
Loans repurchased from the trust — unit count, aggregate UPB, and loan-level detail consistent with the DQ sections.
Monthly Add-On — Time-Series Trend Charts
Sent monthly alongside the weekly report: (1) Actual vs. Theoretical Trust Balance, (2) DQ 60+ Rate & Default Rate over time, (3) Cumulative Turnover since trust inception.

create or replace table marts.kpi.kpi__dscr_underwriting as (
with cte as (
    select
        new_termloanid          as guid,
        new_statusname          as status,
        statuscodename,
        new_activedealstagename as deal_stage,
        new_subject             as subject,
        new_uwlookupname        as uw,
        owneridname             as lo,
        new_processorname       as processor,
        new_targetloanbuyername as target_loan_buyer,
        cast(new_trueclosingdate as date) as closing_date,
        new_totalloanamount     as loan_amount
    from staging.dynamics.term
    where
        (new_statusname = 'Won' and new_trueclosingdate >= '2025-01-01')
        or (
            statuscodename != 'Inactive'
            and new_statusname != 'Lost'
            and new_activedealstagename in (
                'Processing','Initial Underwriting',
                'Conditionally Approved','Final Underwriting','Clear To Close'
            )
        )
    order by closing_date desc
),

select
    from_utc_timestamp(least(
        (select max(createdon) from staging.dynamics.audit),
        (select max(pulled_on) from staging.dynamics.term)
    ), 'America/New_York') as last_update,
    cte.*,
    fl.1st_lts_sent_date        as 1st_lts_sent,
    fi.1st_initial_uw_mark_date as 1st_initial_uw,
    bi.date_stage_before_initial_uw,
    bi.new_stage                as stage_before_initial_uw,
    ff.1st_final_uw_mark_date   as 1st_final_uw,
    bf.date_stage_before_final_uw,
    bf.new_stage                as stage_before_final_uw
from cte
left join first_lts_sent    as fl on fl.objectid = cte.guid
left join first_initial_uw  as fi on fi.objectid = cte.guid
left join first_final_uw    as ff on ff.objectid = cte.guid
left join before_initial_uw as bi on bi.objectid = cte.guid
left join before_final_uw   as bf on bf.objectid = cte.guid
where fl.1st_lts_sent_date >= '2024-06-01'
order by 1st_final_uw desc
)

select
    from_utc_timestamp(least(
        (select max(createdon) from staging.dynamics.audit),
        (select max(pulled_on) from staging.dynamics.term)
    ), 'America/New_York') as last_update,
    user.title, user.department,
    cte.*, tlb.*,
    pr.processor_first_assigned_date,
    moved_to_processing.first_moved_to_processing_date as moved_to_processing_date,
    uw_date.uw_assignments                             as uw_change_count,
    uw_date.uw_first_assigned_date                     as initial_uw_first_assigned_date,
    cond_approved_date.first_cond_approved_date        as cond_approved_first_date
from cte
left join target_loan_buyer   as tlb on tlb.objectid               = cte.guid
left join processor           as pr  on pr.objectid                = cte.guid
left join uw_date                    on uw_date.objectid            = cte.guid
left join cond_approved_date         on cond_approved_date.objectid  = cte.guid
left join moved_to_processing        on moved_to_processing.objectid = cte.guid
left join staging.facts.user         on tlb.system_user_id           = user.dynamics_id
order by tlb.buyer_change_date desc, cte.subject
)

Core tools used across ABL's reporting suite.

Power Query
Automated data extraction from Dynamics CRM and Dropbox-hosted Excel files. Central to the Daily Liquidity Report automation.
VBA Macros
Automates multi-step workflows — consolidating Power Query refreshes into one-click execution for end users.
Excel Formulas & Referencing
Cross-sheet references drive leverage ratios, PVE, covenant thresholds, haircut projections, and 29-day liquidity forecasts.
Databricks SQL
Builds and maintains mart-layer tables via multi-CTE queries against the CRM staging layer. Powers all Power BI dashboards.
Power BI
Connects to Databricks mart tables to surface KPI dashboards for the DSCR UW team.
Dynamics CRM
ABL's primary source for loan-level data: originations, balances, payoffs, and collateral attributes.
Manual Entry
Required for data from external parties — bank portals, Mizuho emails, PDF notices, and Teams posts.

Key terms used across ABL's treasury and finance reports.

TermDefinition
WH / WarehouseShort-term credit facility from a bank used to fund loan originations before they are sold or securitized.
PVEPurchase Value Excess — amount by which collateral value exceeds the outstanding warehouse balance.
UPBUnpaid Principal Balance — remaining principal owed on a loan or pool of loans.
RTL ShelfResidential Transition Loan securitization shelf — issues ABS backed by short-term bridge/fix-and-flip loans.
DSCRDebt Service Coverage Ratio — loan type for investment properties assessed on rental income.
WACWeighted Average Coupon — the weighted average interest rate across loans in the pool.
WALTVWeighted Average Loan-to-Value — weighted average LTV across loans in the pool.
WALPWeighted Average Loan Points — weighted average origination points across the pool.
Sub-LimitTrust-level concentration caps on loan type, LTV, state, borrower, occupancy, and other collateral parameters.
DQ 30+ / 60+ / 90+Days delinquent buckets — loans 30, 60, or 90+ days past their interest payment due date.
TurnoverCumulative UPB of loans that have paid off from the trust since inception.
Initial UWFirst underwriting review stage. First date marked captured as 1st_initial_uw.
Final UWFinal underwriting stage before a loan clears to close. First date marked captured as 1st_final_uw.
PA / AAPayment Account / Accumulation Account — trust accounts within a securitization structure.
1_PagerThe master summary tab/output of the Daily Liquidity Report.
